Cardboard Box

Game Genre: Strategy, Stealth

Game Engine: Unity


Embark on a stealthy adventure in "Cardboard Box," an immersive first-person experience set within the confines of a deserted military base.


Roles & Responsibilities

Responsibilities:

  • Game Design: Crafted gameplay mechanics emphasizing deliberate and calculated strategies, encouraging players to navigate the environment with precision.
  • Level Design: Designed interactive environments filled with obstacles and challenges, requiring players to think creatively to overcome them.
  • Narrative Design: Developed a charming storyline highlighting the joy of exploration and the power of creativity within a modern military setting.
  • Collaboration: Worked closely with artists and sound designers to ensure that the game's visuals and audio complement the stealthy gameplay experience.

Contributions:
  • Central Mechanics: Designed the cardboard box as a versatile tool for evasion, cover, and platforming, providing players with unique gameplay opportunities.
  • Gameplay Loop: Created a tension-filled cycle of surveillance, planning, execution, and evasion, ensuring that each playthrough offers a fresh and engaging experience.
  • Obstacles and Challenges: Implemented security measures such as cameras, laser grids, and traps, challenging players to use their wits to progress through the levels.
  • Level Walkthrough: Designed intricate levels with diverse challenges, each requiring strategic thinking and careful execution to overcome.
  • Aesthetics and Inspiration: Drew inspiration from classic stealth franchises like Splinter Cell, infusing the game with modern military aesthetics and innovative mechanics.
